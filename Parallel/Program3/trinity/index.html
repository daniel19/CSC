The Trinity University Computer Science Department, founded in the spring of 1970, offered the first undergraduate computer science degrees in the Southwest. This widely recognized program offers a Bachelor of Science degree in computer science as well as a minor in management information systems and an honor's program in computer science. The Computer Science Department WEB Pages follow the Trinity University Copyright Policy

For more information about the computer science program, send inquiry to the Computer Science Department Chair.
